Visit Mars

Mars is a terrestrial planet with a thin atmosphere
and has surface features such as impact craters, valleys, dunes and polar ice caps.

Top three activities to do at Mars

Olympus Mons is a large shield volcano on Mars.
It is about two and a half times Mount Everest's height above sea level.

The Martian soil is slightly alkaline and has a basic pH of
7.7 and contains 0.6% of the salt perchlorate, concentrations that are toxic to humans.

The large canyon, Valles Marineris has a length of 4000
kilometres and a depth of up to 7 kilometres. The length is equivalent to the length of Europe.
